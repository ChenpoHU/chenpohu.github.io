risk analysis in software startups

Risk analysis in software startups is the next step after risk identification in software startups and the second step of risk management.

Goals (statistical analysis and qualitave analysis):

 Find out, how strong is the survival rate of the startups influenced by the different factors ---Regression analysis (Dummy Analysis), Discriminant function analysis etc.

 Do a factor analysis to decrease the numbers of the influencing factors

 Do a cluster analysis to find out the similarity and difference between the research objects(software startups), to understand the causes behind the consequences deeper

 Analyse the causes of risks to be able to prepare for the next step of risk management

 Analyse the consequences of the risks in detail

 What is the likelihood that those risks will happen

 Visualisation of the results of the risk analysis with the level of measurement

...

Process:

1. Define the scope and depth of the research and other foundations & concepts

2. Do the research design considering the results of the risk identification model

3. Design the research tools ( questionnaire etc. ) for the date collection

4. Analyse the date with the statistic tools – SPSS and Stata

5. Validity check and usability check of the research system and date

6. Results Analysis

7. Analyse the causes, consequences of the risks
