Start Tour in Israel

As a member of Start Berlin, I help to organise differient events to connect startups, founders, entrepreneurs and students.

We organised the students in Berlin a travel with START Berlin to Tel Aviv and let them join our exciting Israel Entrepreneurship Tour. The 5 day program had introduced the students to the Israeli start-up scene. Our partner, the College of Management Academic Studies, had helped to finalizing the schedule – including a trip to Jerusalem!

The startup ecosystem of Tel Aviv is one of the biggest in the world. The main difference to Berlin is the focus of the companies: Israel's startups are more hich-tech and IoT companies, while Berlin is focusing on rapidly growing E-commerce businesses. We visited Roomer and FairFly, who disrupt the travel market with data and market access, The Junction Tel Aviv's world-famous accelerator program and coworking spaces like Merkspace, Power-ball פאוורבול and SOSA. They host startups like Spitball and Slyde that yet have to raise money and become famous.Amazingly, lots of founders choose to build a startup direct after the serving of the army in Israel. Israel founders are really very brave and have the tendency to take more risks, just as what people always say.

Two coolest Organisations that we visited in Israel:

Tech For Good

- Tech for Good is a place, where people with a big pocket help those with a big heart. That sounds too idealistic to be the truth? Tech For Good Israel proves it to be true. Tech For Good brings people the human side of technology for social change, telling the stories of how people are using technology to make their lives better.Uniting venture capitalists, mentors and promising social enterpreneurs they transform technologies into the means of help for children, seniors and other challenged categories.

Taboola

- One of the biggest Israeli startups Taboola​ analyse the preferences of the users and do successful content marketing and storytelling. They showed us how they know the mystery of snapism today and avoid Blindness "bust" banner to achieve millions of profit providing users with targeted information. This makes the users just enjoy the content that they consume daily and help the customers to more efficiently and as well as effectively to do their marketing.
