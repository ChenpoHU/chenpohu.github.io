The Road to the Summit – Pitching Competition Call for Startups

Are you a high-tech miracle creator or an innovative entrepreneur? Are you looking for high-grade investors or want to raise the profile of your company in the Berlin Startup Scene? Then apply for the Road To The Summit Pitch Competition on February 14th 2017.

Why you should pitch at the event

Success or Failure of a young company is often determined by the network you have. Especially in high-tech areas it is crucial to raise your profile and be known. Pitch in front of a crowd of 150 tech-savvy entrepreneurs and members of the startup scene. Meet Business partners, investors and employees and get feedback from a high-quality jury. Win direct seats to the pitch competition at Start Summit, Europe’s leading Entrepreneurship Conference, March 24/25 in St. Gallen, Switzerland. At the Summit you will present in front of grade-A investors and have a chance to win a five-figure prize.

Who are we looking for:

We are looking for startups in the following areas or for companies that apply these technologies in other markets:

► Blockchain

► Augmented & Virtual Reality

► Aritificial Intelligence

► Internet of Things

Ideally your Startup is younger than 2 years, and has so far only raised a seed round or no money at all. Please note that you will have to pitch in English

Application Process:

If you have a startup in one of our target areas and would like to pitch, please

1. Contact daniel.wenzel@start-berlin.com in German or English. Please send us a pitch deck (it does not have to be the final one) or the URL to your homepage in the Email.

2. After reviewing your project, we will invite you to a short phone call to ask you a few more questions and get an impression of you and your team.

The Format

There will be 6 Startups which will be pitching in two blocks of 3 startups each. Pitches will be 3 minutes long + 3 minutes Q&A by the jury.

Questions?

We welcome you to contact our event manager Daniel Wenzel if you have any questions or comments. Contact: Daniel.Wenzel@start-berlin.com

Presented by

This event is organized by START Berlin, a student entrepreneurship club and the German startups association in cooperation with START Summit.
